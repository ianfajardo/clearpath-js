ClearPath Strategies’ expertise is rooted in global politics, policy and issue advocacy campaigns. We design public opinion research to serve as the foundation of a winning campaign strategy. The research and strategy we have provided politicians, advocacy groups, and NGOs have helped win elections, develop policy positions, and engage the public, on city, state, national and international levels.

Expanding from our political roots, we work extensively with corporate and nonprofit organizations. We help win campaigns, whether they are at the ballot box, legislative sessions, or boardroom, helping leaders tackle some of the most pressing issues of our time like access to affordable healthcare, climate change and sustainability, civil liberties, and many more global issues.

We enhance the way tech thinks by offering our expertise in research on emerging technology trends and issues. On behalf of organizations like Cloud Foundry, Google, and OpenStack, we have conducted research on a variety of forward-looking topics—the future of cloud computing, impending software developer skills shortage, IT infrastructure, open source, API ecosystems, AI transformations, and DevOps implementation best practices. Our findings and recommendations are always data-driven and honest in order to help our clients unlock their blind spots to make big changes.

The ClearPath Strategies team serves leaders in domestic and international politics, corporate and non-profit organizations, tech, and in the environmental and sustainability space. Our cross-domain expertise and market position enable us to connect how the world works today with what it will become.
